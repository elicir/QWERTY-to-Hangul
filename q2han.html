Converting Hangul to Alpha:
On some IMEs such as on Mac, pressing the keys corresponding to ㅂㅈㄷㄱㅅ (qwert) twice in a row will group them together into a double consonant (Select "Repeat").
On others the double consonant can only be accessed by holding shift (Select "Capitalize").
Please note, if "Repeat" is selected, you cannot convert any of "ㅃㅉㄸㄲㅆ" into capital letters.
